Platform overview

Platform overview

Platform Architecture and the Opportunity Engine work together to move from raw dealership records to per-recipient action. AutoCDP positions the platform as an independent execution layer that reads source systems, scores opportunity, launches campaigns, and writes outcomes back without forcing stores to abandon the tools they already run.

Platform architecture

The whitepaper frames AutoCDP as an agentic AI platform for automotive retail built around an execution layer rather than a passive reporting layer. The operating model is designed to connect DMS and CRM data, route opportunities into execution, and expose the full system to executive oversight.

Opportunity Engine

The Opportunity Engine is described as per-recipient, meaning each customer is evaluated individually instead of being pushed through broad static lists. That architecture matters because the platform is supposed to decide who should receive what message, through which channel, and at what moment.

Compliance & Learning Spine

The platform is governed by a structural Compliance & Learning Spine, not by one final review step. That means compliance gates and learning logic are built into the operating system itself instead of being bolted on after creative is generated.

Technical differentiator

The key claim is not just unification. It is that the platform can identify opportunity, enforce compliance by construction, launch execution, and then improve the next cycle through the Learning Ledger.
