AI direct mail

The whitepaper names the Direct Mail Engine as a first-class part of the architecture, not a side feature. That matters because physical mail is treated as part of the same operating system as digital channels and attribution.

Direct Mail Engine

Direct mail appears as its own dedicated section in the technical whitepaper. That positioning signals that mail is part of the core system architecture rather than an optional add-on.

Per-recipient execution

Because the wider platform is built around a per-recipient Opportunity Engine, direct mail is framed as something that should be selected deliberately for the right customer at the right moment, not blasted generically.

Compliance-aware delivery

The mail workflow sits inside the same compliance-by-construction environment as the rest of the platform. That means direct mail logic is supposed to respect the same structural controls as any other campaign path.

Measured contribution

Direct mail also belongs to the closed-loop model, which means it should feed response data back into the Learning Ledger so the system can learn whether physical outreach outperformed or complemented digital channels.
