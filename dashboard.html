Three things AutoCDP does that Fullpath can't
Data unification is table stakes. These are the execution capabilities that change what a dealership can actually do.
Execution — Every campaign you should run but aren't

Most dealers know they have lapsed customers. AutoCDP doesn't have a capacity problem. Six agents run 24/7 — every signal triggers a campaign, every customer gets contacted. No manual work, no backlog.

Attribution — Direct mail with a paper trail

AI-written, handwriting-simulated postcards with a unique QR code on every single piece. When a customer scans it, you know exactly who, when, and which campaign closed the appointment — not a modeled estimate, not a batch open rate.

Independence — Your CRM investment stays protected

Fullpath is now owned by Cox — the same company that sells VinSolutions to your dealers. AutoCDP is independent. We read from your CRM, execute campaigns, and write outcomes back. BDC reps see every touch without switching tools.

Closed-Loop Learning Ledger
After every send, outcomes reconcile against each creative variant. Winning angles promote; weak ones retire.
