Three things AutoCDP does that Fullpath can't

Execution — every campaign you should run but aren't

Six agents run 24/7. Every signal triggers a campaign, every customer gets contacted. No capacity problem, no manual work, no backlog.

Attribution — direct mail with a paper trail

AI-written, handwriting-simulated postcards with a unique QR code on every piece. When scanned you know who, when, and which campaign closed the appointment.

Independence — your CRM investment stays protected

Fullpath is owned by Cox — which also sells VinSolutions. AutoCDP is independent: we read, execute, and write outcomes back. Nothing competes with what you paid for.

Closed-Loop Learning Ledger

After every send, outcomes reconcile against each creative variant. Winning angles promote to anchors; weak ones retire.
Promote what works

Proven anchors feed straight back into the Creative Agent's next campaign — results compound instead of resetting.

Retire what doesn't

Underperforming variants are retired automatically by the Learning & Optimization Agent.

Network effect

Every campaign across every dealer teaches the system. A dealer who joins today inherits learning from millions of campaigns they never ran.

Orchestrator Agent
Plans and coordinates the entire execution pipeline. Decomposes a plain-language campaign goal into structured subtasks, routes work to each specialist, tracks shared state across all six agents, handles retries, and decides when to re-run on new DMS data.
Execution planAgent routing decisionsRe-run scheduling

Sonnet 4.6
Data Intelligence Agent
Computes per-customer churn probability, lifetime-value estimate, lifecycle stage, and channel fit from repair-order history, recency, vehicle age, and behavioral signals — returning a ranked audience with confidence scores.
Scored customer listChurn & LTV rankingLifecycle classification
Agent 03
Sonnet 4.6
Audience Targeting Agent
Applies channel-fit scoring from the global learnings database, assigning each customer a single channel or a sequenced multi-channel plan with conditional triggers — then generates the execution plan the downstream agents act against.
Per-customer channel selectionSequenced timing logicFinal audience plan
Agent 04
Sonnet 4.6
Creative Generation Agent
Generates unique content per recipient from their vehicle, mileage, service history, and current offer — postcard, SMS, and email. No template strings: Opportunity-Score modulation and a generation-time FCRA gate shape every piece as it is written.
Per-recipient postcard copySMS + email variantsOpportunity-modulated messaging
Agent 05
Haiku 4.5
Compliance Review Agent
Runs after creative generation and before any send, validating each message against federal and state rules. Any failure halts that recipient and records a structured reason. Runs on Haiku 4.5 for sub-200ms deterministic checks.
